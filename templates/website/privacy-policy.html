Introduction

Welcome to the Penguin Bot! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our bot on Discord. By using Penguin Bot, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Data: When you interact with Penguin Bot, we may collect data such as your Discord username, user ID, and any messages you send to the bot.
  • Message Content: If you choose to use features like venting, the content of your messages will be stored and may be displayed in designated channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use the information collected to operate Penguin Bot and provide you with the features you request, such as anonymous venting.

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel. We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

  • With Discord: Your information is shared with Discord to facilitate the functionality of the bot.
  • With Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties without your consent, except as required by law.

5. User Rights

You have the right to request access to the information we hold about you, to request corrections, or to request deletion of your data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the bot's documentation. Your continued use of Penguin Bot after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
